About US

LeadLeap Consulting Limited is a training and consulting firm which provide Microsoft Office Training and Consultancy for individuals & Companies. LCL has experience in training small and large groups across diverse industries. Proven success in leveraging educational theories and methodologies to design, develop, and deliver successful training programs and integrate instructional technology to provide onsite and virtual training. Adept at organizing and facilitating Microsoft Office, Management and Team building training programs and activities Lead-Leap is passionate about helping businesses; both new and existing to develop winning strategies, offering insightful ideas, training, recommending, developing, and deploying IT based solutions that will improve productivity and profitability of the organizations. The Lead-Leap team is constituted by young, vibrant, experienced and upwardly mobile professionals in the field of training and development, marketing and information technology, who are poised to see organisations grow and improve their performances, and to also compete favorably with other brands anywhere across the globe
